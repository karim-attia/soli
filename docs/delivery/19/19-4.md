 ## Requirements
 - Cover enabling/disabling statistics toggles and confirm persisted state across app reloads.
 - Verify that the HUD displays move count and/or timer only when toggles are enabled.
 - Assert that the timer starts after the first move, remains idle beforehand, and stops after game completion.
 - Confirm that starting a new game reveals the first stock card while leaving timer at zero until the first move.
 - Capture screenshots or logs as evidence for review where applicable.

 4. Automate persistence checks by restarting the app within the test flow.
 5. Document test results and attach artefacts for review.
